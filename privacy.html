Data Privacy

Effective date: 2025-11-11

Your privacy is our priority. Authority is designed so that your personal information is never collected, stored, or sold.

Anonymous Participation

You vote on figures and participate in polls without providing personal details. Your device fingerprint is used only for tracking votes and claiming Authority, and does not identify you personally.

Device Fingerprint Usage

To enable anonymous participation and track your actions, we generate a device fingerprint using hardware and software characteristics. This fingerprint is not linked to any personal information and is only used within the app.

No Personal Data Storage

We do not collect your name, email, or any other identifying information. All votes and actions are recorded using anonymous device fingerprints.

Wallet Connections

You can connect any wallet (Valora recommended, MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.) to claim Authority for your votes. Wallet addresses are used only to process claims and are not linked to your real-world identity.

No Mnemonic or Private Key Storage

Authority does NOT generate wallets for you. If you connect an external wallet, none of your wallet's private information or mnemonic is stored or transmitted by the app.

Blockchain Transparency

All claiming and balance transactions are recorded on the blockchain, which is public and transparent by design. However, these records are not linked to your real-world identity by Authority.

No Data Selling

We never sell, share, or monetize your data. Your participation and sentiment remain private and secure.

Questions

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please contact us through the app support channels.
